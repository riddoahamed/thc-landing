Our planet pledge

We sell water.
We protect water.
No exceptions.

Bangladesh's rivers are who we are. The families who still can't drink clean water in 2026 are who we're accountable to. We can't take from water without giving back to it.

/ 01
Plastic neutral by 2027

For every gram of plastic in our supply chain, we collect and recycle at least one gram from Bangladesh's rivers, waterways and streets. Numbers published quarterly. No glossing.

/ 02
River & waterway cleanups

Monthly cleanup drives across Dhaka's canals. Growing nationwide. We recruit locally, pay community teams, track plastic removed by weight. No optics. Just work.

/ 03
Clean water for underserved families

A portion of every product sold goes toward clean water access for Bangladeshi families who still don't have it. We partner with local NGOs. No overhead theatre.

/ 04
Glass and aluminum first, always

Our core line never ships in single-use plastic. When plastic appears in the chain, we offset it, replace it, and account for it publicly. Every time.

04 / What we stand for

Six things.
Zero
compromises.

Not values on a wall. Rules that cost us something. We chose them anyway.

/ 01
Honest labelsIf we wouldn't drink it ourselves, it doesn't go in the bottle. No filler, no fake flavour, no asterisks.
